Space Trivia Game Instructions

  1. Objective: The goal of the game is to answer as many space-related questions correctly as possible to achieve a high score.
  2. Starting the Game: Click on the "Start" button to begin the trivia.
  3. Answering Questions: Select your answer from the provided options.
  4. Feedback on Answers: You will receive immediate feedback on whether your answer is correct or incorrect.
  5. Next Question: Click the "Next Question" button to move on to the next question.
  6. Viewing Your Score: Your total score will be displayed after answering all questions.
  7. Restarting the Game: Click the "Restart Game" button to reset the game and play again.
  8. Enjoy and Learn: Have fun while learning interesting facts about space!
